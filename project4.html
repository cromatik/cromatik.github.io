04. Tropical fish tank Paper wall > Anna Puig Interiorisme
— Paper wall designer for the kitchen of an international consultant in Barcelona.

Challenge

  • Big kitchen in a new refurbished apartment in Barcelona it was too cold in colour
  • The kitchen is one and it is one of the key elements of the apartment
  • Highly prestigious Interior designer ask us for a “point of colour”

Aproach

  • We design and produce a very colourful wall paper including plants and fish following the owner strong interest in plants and the sea
  • In order to present the draft to the owner and interior designer we developed an animated video to show the “life” inside of the design
  • The kitchen has now a new life and it has become the centre of the house
