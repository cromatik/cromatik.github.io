01. Get guidance to make moves on your goals Visual identity > Fitbit
— Fitbit motivates you to reach your health and fitness goals by tracking your activity, exercise, sleep, weight and more.

Challenge

  • Develop a point of sales display which produces great visibility of their products
  • No much exhibition space
  • Periodic change of the content to be included in the display
  • Strong and easy to assemble by the employees of the retail (Fnac, Mediamarkt)

Aproach

  • We design a metallic and magnetic body display
  • Panels of information can be easily magnetically attached and change regularly and easy to send
  • Panels of information are designed with the very attractive Fitbit colours and their graphics
  • Display is personalized and designed to cover the needs of the Fitbit products and highlighting the characteristic of each product
  • We created differents materials to promote all of materials
